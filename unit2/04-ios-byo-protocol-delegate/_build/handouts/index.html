Build Your Own App - Protocol & Delegate

Students will build their own app. This app must use the protocol-delegate communication pattern.

You do not have to, but we recommend you model your app after the Star Wars Card Game or Task.

Remember to reach out in the queue for assistance. If you are unsure about your app idea, share it with a SME for feedback.

As usual, this app must be pushed to GitHub. We recommend you create your repository first, and then begin working on your app, committing changes as you go.
